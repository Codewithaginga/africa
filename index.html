OPERATION BEYOND BORDERS

Africa is the second largest Continent in the world with a population of 1.4 billion people.
Our aim is to unite Africa throught achieving a common goal.

MEMBERS

AGINGA MADIANG

Aginga Madiang is the founder and the C.E.O of AFRICAN RELEVANCE. A 23 years old born and raised in a small village in Kenya called Burumba.

"Africa's population presents 16% of the world population. Data state that movements within Africa to Africa countries is less than 40%. This as a result of strigen measures that are based on historical rather than factual. Europeans established these boundaries for us, without our will/consent. And as a result of these divisions we have become our own enemy. Our African economy will never be stable unless we start to operate as one block."
- Mr. Madiang
